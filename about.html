About Me

Hello there! I'm Anson, and my aim is to specialize in UI/UX/Interaction Design as well as front-end development in the future. I find immense satisfaction in refining the way we engage with computers and various interfaces to create seamless and intuitive interactions.

Guided by a commitment to push beyond conventional design boundaries, my path as a UI/UX designer and front-end developer is a dedicated exploration of digital storytelling. My particular passion lies in the seamless integration of form and function, where I thrive on optimizing software and products. Efficiency is not just a goal for me but a must, enhancing experiences through finely tuned design.

My approach is grounded in user research, design thinking, and prototyping, ensuring every facet of design is meticulously crafted. This dedication to innovation and optimization defines my unique perspective as a creator, where efficiency isn't just a necessity but a stimulant to enriching the user journey.

Currently, I'm on the lookout for Co-op and internship opportunities as I pursue my studies in Interactive Arts and Technology and Communication at Simon Fraser University.
